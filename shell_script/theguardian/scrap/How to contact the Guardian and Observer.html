How to contact the Guardian, the Observer and the Guardian website

Please address any correspondence to:
Kings Place, 90 York Way
London N1 9GU

The switchboard number for the Guardian, the Observer and the Guardian website is:

020 3353 2000 or dialling from overseas +4420 3 353 2000

Website and mobile user help at the Guardian
For any problems using the website, our mobile apps and their links, or general questions and feedback about the Guardian network, please contact: userhelp@theguardian.com
If your query relates to the Guardian iPad edition, please email tablet@theguardian.com

To contact editorial departments and desks

See the list below - please take care to send queries to the correct destination as there is no guarantee that messages will be forwarded

To contact any member of staff
Individual staff, whether editorial or commercial, can be contacted using this email format:
Firstname.lastname@theguardian.com
Firstname.lastname@observer.co.uk

Letters to the editor
Letters for publication should be sent to these addresses. The sender's full postal address and phone number are required, for verification only:
Guardian guardian.letters@theguardian.com
Observer observer.letters@observer.co.uk

Corrections and clarifications of editorial content - the readers' editors*
It is the policy of the Guardian and the Observer to correct significant editorial errors and handle editorial complaints as soon as possible. The readers' editors are independent internal ombudsmen and can be contacted as follows:

Chris Elliott - Guardian readers' editor
reader@theguardian.com
Guardian readers' editor page

Stephen Pritchard - Observer readers' editor
reader@observer.co.uk
Observer readers' editor page

[* Please note that queries about subscriptions, vouchers, reader offers, advertising, marketing and other non-editorial areas are not handled by the readers' editors. These should be addressed to the relevant departments (see below). Story suggestions should also be sent to the relevant departments.]

Freelance contributions

If you would like to contribute freelance content, please ensure you have read our Contributors' guide.

Any queries you have relating to the Freelance Charter or Rights/permissions should be directed to our Rights Department at rights@theguardian.com

Employment and work experience

Getting the Guardian abroad

The availability of the Guardian and Observer in newsagents is now very limited outside of the UK. However, you can still purchase these publications in a small selection of countries and cities or you can subscribe and have your copy delivered to your door.

For more information on purchasing copies of the Guardian abroad, please contact the international publishing team.
