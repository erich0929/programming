iPhone 6-friendly clothes – in pictures

They’ve changed the way we talk, shop, eat, run and even ovulate. Now smartphones are changing the way we dress. The announcement that Apple is launching a new iPhone 6, possibly on 9 September, is exciting news. Except for one detail – they’re too big for our pockets. The two new handsets will have screens 4.7in wide and 5.5in high. If you work in metric, that can be loosely converted to ‘pretty big’. With that in mind, we’ve picked a few pieces for men and women with very big pockets. Buy now, you’ll thank us later
