Title :

I want to end my loveless marriage, but I am financially dependent

My wife of 25 years takes me for granted and belittles me, but I don't think she would accept a split. I am desperate for love and companionship. What can I do?

Contributor :

Public Date :

Friday 8 August 2014 15.44 BST

Content :

My 25-year marriage has been a loveless mistake. My high-earning wife holds the cards. I am mostly the housekeeper, which is fine, but I am taken for granted, belittled, ignored, and disrespected. I know she is unhappy too, and I would prefer just to calmly discuss with her our need for a total break. But she is deeply dishonest with herself and others, and her response is likely to be catastrophic. I have no personal resources or support network whatever. What to do? I am absolutely desperate for love, affection, touch, companionship, conversation. I am a fit 58, a decent human being, and I want to live.
